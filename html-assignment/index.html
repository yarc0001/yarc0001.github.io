About me

I’m Mateo, a 28-year-old Colombian that recently moved to Canada. I studied International Business before and graduated in 2018. The business field allowed me to work in great places and meet amazing people; however, I was not particularly happy about the things I was doing, that’s when I decided to change my life and start from scratch in a new country. Canada has been the place to get to know me better and explore new passions. This journey has not been an easy one, the hardest part would be leaving my family and my cat Tila. However, I’m confident that once I am settled here, I’ll bring her with us. This decision was possible because I moved here with my partner, and it’s been an enormous support to have each other during this challenging process. I’m enjoying Canadian winter, but cannot wait to leave my house without 3 layers of clothes on.
