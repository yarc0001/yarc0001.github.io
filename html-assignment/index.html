About me

I am Mateo, a 28-year-old Colombian that recently moved to Canada. I studied International Business before and graduated in August 28th,2018. The business field allowed me to work in great places and meet amazing people; however, I was not particularly happy about the things I was doing, that is when I decided to change my life and start from scratch in a new country. Canada has been the place to get to know me better and explore new passions.

This journey has not been an easy one, the hardest part would have been leaving my family and my cat Tila. Nevertheless, I am confident that once I am settled here, I will bring our kitty daugther with us. This decision was possible because I moved here with my partner, it has been an enormous support to have each other during this challenging process; specially during winter. I am enjoying the experience of the Canadian winter, but cannot wait to leave my house without 3 layers of clothes on.

The first step towards getting somewhere is to decide that you are not going to stay where you are. JP Morgan
